Serena Williams

Serena Williams, the African American tennis superstar retired this past weekend.
She is widely considered to be the geatest female athlete in history.
Alongside her sister Venus, she helped revolutionise the game and world of tennis, making it accessible to a wider audience.

3 Great Facts about Serena Williams:

  1. She won the Golden Slam in both singles and doubles, which was appropriately known as The Serena Slam
  2. She is the highest earning female athlete of all time
  3. She was 8 weeks pregnant with her daughter Olympia when she won the Australian Open in 2017
